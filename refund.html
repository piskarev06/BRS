Refund

A refund is possible if:

  • the product was not in use
  • its presentation and consumer properties have been preserved
  • attached is a document confirming the fact and conditions of purchase of the specified product with a completed return form, which is attached to the parcel.

The products supplied in the kit are accepted only in the kit. If the Buyer refuses the goods, the Seller returns to him the amount paid by the Buyer in accordance with the contract, with the exception of the Seller's expenses for the delivery of the returned goods from the Buyer, no later than 14 days from the date of the Buyer's submission of the relevant claim. If a dispute arises about the causes of the defect during the warranty period, the store is obliged to conduct an examination of the product at its own expense. If, as a result of the examination, it is established that the defects arose due to circumstances for which the seller is not responsible (including due to violations of the rules of operation, storage and care of the product), the Buyer is obliged to reimburse the seller for the costs of the examination. Return the products to the address specified in the return request.

According to article 26.1, the consumer has the right to refuse the goods at any time before its transfer, and after the transfer of the goods - within seven days. If the consumer refuses the goods, the seller must return to him the amount of money paid by the consumer under the contract, with the exception of the seller's expenses for the delivery of the returned goods from the consumer, no later than ten days from the date of the consumer's presentation of the corresponding claim.

According to Article 19, paragraph 1 of the "Law on Consumer Rights Protection", the Consumer has the right to submit the requirements provided for in Article 18 of this Law to the seller (manufacturer, authorized organization or authorized individual entrepreneur, importer) regarding the defects of the goods, if they are found during the warranty period or expiration date.

According to article 19, paragraph 2 of the "Law on Consumer Protection", the warranty period of the goods, as well as its service life, is calculated from the date of transfer of the goods to the consumer, unless otherwise provided by the contract. If it is impossible to establish the date of transfer, these terms are calculated from the date of manufacture of the goods.

According to Article 19, paragraph 6 "On Consumer Rights Protection", after the expiration of the warranty period (30 days from the date of receipt of the goods), the burden of proving the presence of a manufacturing defect of the goods (examination) lies with the consumer. For seasonal goods (shoes, clothing, etc.) these terms are calculated from the moment of the onset of the corresponding season, the date of which is determined respectively by the subjects of the Russian Federation based on the climatic conditions of the location of consumers.

According to Article 18, paragraph 5 "On Consumer Rights Protection", the seller (manufacturer), an authorized organization or an authorized individual entrepreneur, importer are obliged to accept goods of improper quality from the consumer and, if necessary, to check the quality of the goods. The consumer has the right to participate in the quality control of the goods.

In the event of a dispute about the causes of the defects of the goods, the seller (manufacturer), an authorized organization or an authorized individual entrepreneur, importer are obliged to conduct an examination of the goods at their own expense. The examination of the goods is carried out within the time limits established by Articles 20, 21 and 22 of this Law to meet the relevant requirements of the consumer. The consumer has the right to be present during the examination of the goods and, in case of disagreement with its results, to challenge the conclusion of such an examination in court.

If, as a result of the examination of the goods, it is established that its shortcomings arose as a result of circumstances for which the seller (manufacturer) is not responsible, the consumer is obliged to reimburse the seller (manufacturer), an authorized organization or an authorized individual entrepreneur, the importer for the costs of conducting the examination, as well as the costs associated with its storage and transportation of the goods."

It is not possible to exchange the product for the same one, but of a different color or size. But you can return what did not fit, and make a new order in the online store. Please note, the price of the product may differ from the original.
